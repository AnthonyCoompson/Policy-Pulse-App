Workflow
From news to
briefing in minutes

PolicyPulse replaces hours of manual monitoring with an automated intelligence pipeline that surfaces only what matters.

01
Automated Daily Scrape
The backend scrapes 20+ sources at 7am daily. Your keyword watchlist drives additional Google News searches across the internet.
02
AI Relevance Filtering
Groq AI scores each article 1–10 for relevance to your field. Only articles scoring 6+ are stored — eliminating irrelevant noise automatically.
03
Review & Curate
Articles appear in your feed with AI summaries, sentiment tags, and “Why It Matters” analysis. Stage key articles to your digest with one click.
04
Publish Your Briefing
Export a formatted PDF briefing note, send by email to subscribers, or share a read-only public link — all from the Digest tab.

About PolicyPulse
Built from the
inside of policy work

PolicyPulse was built out of a direct need — the gap between the volume of policy information being published daily across BC and Canadian government sources, and the time available to monitor and synthesize it.

Policy professionals shouldn't spend their days doing what a well-designed system can do in seconds.

Every feature in PolicyPulse reflects a real workflow pain point: the time spent searching multiple ministry websites, manually formatting citations, assembling digest emails, or missing a key announcement buried in a Hansard release.
